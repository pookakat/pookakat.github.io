My ability to communicate to a vast array of people, from senior developers to product owners gave me a firm foundation towards testing new applications for Scripps' various web sites and advertising campaigns. I also assisted in migrating all content from the in-house CMS to a more modern and easy to develop AWS environment.

It was at Scripps that I found my true love towards web development. After completing my contract, my husband and I moved back to the Raleigh-Durham area, and the search to return to a more technical role began. Soon, we found our way to UNC's Coding Bootcamp, and my journey to become a developer truly began.

Detail driven, team-oriented, with a growth mindset, I went to UNC's Coding Bootcamp to hone my skills further develop my knowledge to become Super Developer, defender of semantics and champion of proper grammar.
